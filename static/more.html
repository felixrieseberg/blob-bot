More Information

Kona is a Slack app that posts activities from Strava clubs to a Slack channel and allows users to see, query, and check Strava activities using Slack Slash commands.

Pricing

Kona is free. It is maintained by me, Felix Rieseberg, as a gift to my fellow runners, bikers, and swimmers.

Source Code

Kona is open source. Contributions and issue reports are welcome over on GitHub.

Contact

You can reach me at felix@felixrieseberg.com. If you are about to send me a feature request, please take a minute to consider that you're paying $0 for Kona. Requests that come bundled with as little as a coffee donation do stand a chance though!
